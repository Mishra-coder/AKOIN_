Bridging Academia & Industry

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute for digital, cyber, and technology risk roles in regulated, high-consequence environments.

We emphasize the critical link between academic insight—through our UK university partnerships—and real-world excellence. Our mission is to equip professionals with the resilience needed to navigate complex AI and digital risk landscapes.
